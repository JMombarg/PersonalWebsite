Welcome to my homepage!

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Département d'Astrophysique at CEA Paris-Saclay, France, in the group led by Dr. Stéphane Mathis. My research focusses on stellar evolution of rotating stars. I am involved in the ERC Synergy grant 4D-STAR . The goal of my research is to improve our understanding of stellar structure and evolution of rotating massive stars by developing state-of-the-art 3-D (+1 temporal dimension) stellar structure and evolution models to be coupled to asteroseismic data.
