Welcome to my homepage!

I am a PhD student at the Institute of Astronomy, KU Leuven in Belgium. My research focusses on stellar evolution of single stars. I am involved in the ERC advanced grant MAMSIE (Mixing and Angular Momentum tranSport of massIvE stars) led by Prof. Conny Aerts. The goal of the MAMSIE project is to improve our understanding of stellar structure and evolution by making use of observed stellar pulsations which allow us to study the deep interior of stars.
