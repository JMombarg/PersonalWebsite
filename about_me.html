About me

I am a Dutch astrophysicist and I have completed my BSc and MSc in Physics and Astronomy at the Radboud University Nijmegen (The Netherlands), graduating with distinction. I was awarded the `De Zeeuw-Van Dishoeck' 2018 prize, given out for exceptional work on an astronomy MSc thesis. In February 2022, I obtained my PhD entitled Asteroseismic modelling of intermediate-mass stars under the supervision of Prof. Conny Aerts and Dr. Timothy Van Reeth at KU Leuven in Belgium. During my PhD, I have won two best-poster awards and was awarded a 9-month travel grant (ultimately shortened to 6.5 months due to the COVID pandemic) from the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO) to carry out my proposal to do 2D stellar modelling at the Institut de Recherche en Astrophysique et Planétologie in Toulouse, France.
